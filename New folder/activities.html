Enjoy our full range of activities at Rivercove Inn

What can I look forward to?
Our hotel has a beautiful swimming pool and a gymnasium to ensure you don't miss your workouts when on vacation. Hit our game room and plug in the console of your choice Or you could take your turn at miniature golf, basketball, table tennis, pool, and more!
What about seeing the sights around?
We offer a free tour of the surrounding area for our visitors. Book a tour at least two nights before to help us plan it better. The beautiful surrounds make the experience even more interesting.
What I need to carry a lot of things with me?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
